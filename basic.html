College Days

Night of the Knights- Kaashiyatra!

Attending a DJ night at Kaashiyatra, IIT BHU, was an unforgettable experience. The energy was incredible, and the crowd was full of enthusiastic students, including myself, ready to have a great time. The DJ played a mix of popular tracks that kept everyone dancing and singing along. The light show added to the vibrant atmosphere, creating a memorable night with my friends. It was a great way to relax and enjoy the music after a day of events and activities at the festival. Of course, the cultural experience was thrilling, but the songs had most of my favor, and fervour too!

Day Outs in Streets of Varanasi

After classes, my friends and I often made impromptu trips to explore Varanasi and its ghats. We would stroll along the bustling streets, soaking in the sights and sounds of the city. The ghats along the Ganges River were always a highlight, with their vibrant atmosphere and spiritual significance. We enjoyed watching the beautiful sunset over the river and observing the evening aarti ceremonies, which were mesmerizing. These spontaneous outings allowed us to unwind and bond, creating unforgettable memories of our time in Varanasi..
